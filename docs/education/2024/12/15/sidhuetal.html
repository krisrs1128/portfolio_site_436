Find Your College Match

Author: Harmohini Sidhu, Mihika Vuppula, Sanvi Kumar, Tanvi Kulkarni, Tirzah Asir, Willow Shi

Click here to visit the interactive app.

The team created this interactive app to assist users in exploring and analyzing educational institutions, providing a streamlined experience through a user-friendly multi-tab layout. Each tab is developed to guide users through different stages of the decision-making process:

Tab 1: Broadly explore institutions or locate them based on commuting accommodations.
Tab 2: Screen and filter candidate institutions using key criteria.
Tab 3: Dive deeper into selected institutions for detailed analysis and insights.
